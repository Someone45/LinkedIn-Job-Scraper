Job Description

Are you the future Nick Sullivan, Nick Carvell, or Robert Rabensteiner of fashion journalism? Then keep reading.

The Fashion Journalist interns will cover all men's grooming and style-related content that identifies with and relates to Man Of The Hour's target demographic.

The candidate must have a deep interest in fashion, especially as it relates to menswear.

The candidate must possess an aptitude/interest in writing men's fashion and accessories.

The candidate must possess an aptitude/interest in writing men's skincare and grooming.

Fashion Journalist I

Responsibilities

Digital

Write a bi-weekly fashion editorial, translating runway or lookbook images into wearable items for our readers. Plus, present a luxury special item.

Write a monthly column focusing on menswear news.

Write a monthly column focused on skincare and grooming for men.

Print

Write one fashion editorial and one grooming feature for the annual magazine.

Fashion Journalist II

Responsibilities

Digital

Write a bi-annual review of runway shows from fashion week.

Write regular reviews of lookbook collections.

Print

Write two fashion presentations for our annual magazines.

Duration: 6 months

This is a virtual and unpaid internship. Yet, you will gain valuable writing experience.

Official Start Date: ASAP
