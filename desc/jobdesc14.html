Desc
Garda Capital Partners LP (Garda) is a multi-billion dollar alternative investment firm with over 18 years of experience deploying relative value strategies across fixed income markets for institutional investors. Garda has offices in Wayzata, New York City, West Palm Beach, Geneva, Copenhagen, and Singapore.

Garda's Risk Management team is looking to hire a Risk Analyst Intern that will join our team in the New York Office, for Summer 2023. The intern will be responsible for supporting all aspect of the market risk management function while interacting with traders, risk managers, middle office, and other teams.

Position Responsibilities
Assist in the publishing of risk and P&L metrics of the trading desk
Monitoring and analyzing risk exposures on both a daily and longer-term basis
Understanding and analyzing risk representation of various traded products and other investments
Developing knowledge in the firm's products, trading strategies, and market characteristics
Aid in the preparation of risk reports and presentations and communicate risk factors to broader Risk Management team

Qualifications & Desired Skills
Pursuing Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, or related field
Demonstrated interest in investment management
Proficient with Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint, experience with Bloomberg Terminals is preferred but not required
Detail-oriented nature with strong quantitative orientation and analytical skills
Ability to manage rapidly changing priorities and workloads
Understanding of how to move forward in complex or ambiguous situations
Strong achievement and results orientation
