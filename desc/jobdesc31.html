About Open Road Integrated Media

Open Road Integrated Media is a prestige content brand delivering digital experiences that entertain and inform readers around the world. Laser-focused on revenue, reach, and repeat customers, the Open Road network of digital properties produces compelling stories that keep audiences engaged—across devices, content verticals, and global territories.

Summary

The Managing Editorial Intern will assist the Managing Editorial and Production team with a number of tasks related to ebook and print production, including quality assurance, metadata entry and optimization, updates to ebook files, copyediting, and proofreading. The intern will report to the Managing Editorial Assistant and work closely with Production Editors. This position is unpaid and for-credit only. You must be eligible to receive academic credit.

Open Road is a fast-paced, high-profile startup—details and deadlines are of the essence. The right individual will have the unique opportunity to experience all aspects of managing editorial in a next-generation publishing environment.

Essential Functions
Perform quality assurance of Open Road titles on major reading devices (iPad, Kindle, and others)
Enter descriptive copy and other metadata as needed in title management system
Copyedit and format descriptive copy in accordance with Chicago Manual of Style and ORIM house style
Prep books for digitization and tag manuscripts with preset styles for conversion and/or typesetting
Review print interior files for formatting issues
Convert ebooks from manuscript and assist with formatting and updating ebook files

Requirements
Excellent attention to detail and ability to efficiently manage time and work independently
Demonstrated interest in managing editorial and production for next-generation publishing
Knowledge of e-reader devices and basic grasp of html and InDesign preferred
Must be eligible to receive academic credit for this internship opportunity
Available 2-3 days a week
