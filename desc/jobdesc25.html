Alfred A. Knopf was founded in 1915 and has long been known as a publisher of celebrated and thought-provoking hardcover fiction and non-fiction. Its list of authors includes Toni Morrison, John Updike, Cormac McCarthy, Alice Munro, Anne Rice, Anne Tyler, Jane Smiley, James Baldwin, Richard Ford, Julia Child, Kazuo Ishiguro, and Michael Ondaatje, as well as such classic writers as Thomas Mann, Willa Cather, John Hersey, and John Cheever.

Penguin Random House is seeking a Junior Designer to join the award-winning Knopf Doubleday art department to work across all of our imprints: Knopf, Doubleday, Pantheon, Vintage, and Anchor. Responsibilities include designing and preparing mechanicals for paperback covers and hardcover jackets and designing and creating social media presentations. The Junior Designer will work closely with the art directors and senior design staff, as well as editorial, managing editorial, and production departments to assist in taking book covers through the entire design process. This is an amazing opportunity to join an innovative art department and work with the renowned titles of Knopf.

Responsibilities :
Designing original book covers and jackets
Preparing cover mechanicals
Preparing files for production
Reviewing and correcting proofs with the assistance of senior staff
Utilizing design skills in the modification of layouts and series titles
Adapting hardcover designs for paperback, putting together movie tie-in covers
Making reprint corrections
Designing typography-driven eBook covers
Help design and prepare social media campaigns for Instagram
Assist in brand development

Requirements:
Graphic design degree or relevant work experience
Design internship experience a plus
Genuine interest in book cover design
Experience with motion graphic, photography, and social media a plus
Ability to manage multiple priorities in a deadline-driven environment
Strong organizational skills with firm attention to detail
Ability to think and work creatively
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Strong knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Acrobat)
